Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, who hails from the Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Now is the current chief executive officer of Microsoft. He was appointed as CEO on 4 February 2014.

On his first day in the job, the father-of-three sent an email to all staff, calling it "a very humbling day for me".

"I am... defined by my curiosity and thirst for learning," he told staff. "I buy more books than I can finish. I sign up for more online courses than I can complete.

"I fundamentally believe that if you are not learning new things, you stop doing great and useful things."

Before becoming CEO, he was Executive Vice President of Microsoft's cloud and enterprise group, responsible for building and running the company's computing platforms, developer tools and cloud computing services.
