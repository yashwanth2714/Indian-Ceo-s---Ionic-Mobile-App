Rajeev Vasudeva

Rajeev Vasudeva is CEO of Zurich-based executive search firm Egon Zehnder.

Mr.Vasudeva joined Egon Zehnder in 1995 when his consultancy Sachar Vasudeva & Associates merged with Egon Zehnder.

Mr Vasudeva has a BA in economics from St Stephens College, Delhi, and an MBA from University of Michigan.
